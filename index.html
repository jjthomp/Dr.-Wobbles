Wiring Preparation (JST Connections)

  1. Cut your wires to the appropriate lengths.
  2. Carefully strip the ends of the wires.
  3. Crimp the JST pins onto the exposed wire ends.
  4. Insert the crimped pins into the JST plastic housings.
  5. Solder the opposite ends to the corresponding components (e.g., motor, button) as detailed in Phase 3.

Phase 4: Final Assembly

Follow these steps carefully to assemble the device. Reference the images for exact placement.

1. Materials Overview

Gather all your 3D printed parts, the prepared PCB, battery, motor, and hardware.

2. Elastic Band Placement (For Ice Pack)

Insert the elastic bands through the rear slots to hold the ice pack securely against the back. Tie them securely in a knot.

3. Battery Insertion

Place the battery securely into the lower compartment of the enclosure.

4. Motor Placement

Seat the motor in its cradle mount. Apply a small amount of glue if necessary to prevent rattling.

5. Circuit Placement

Slide the main PCB into the primary slot.

6. Circuit Holder Placement

Position the 3D-printed circuit holder over the PCB to lock it into place.

7. Wire Connections

Connect the JST terminals to the board. Ensure polarities match the diagram.

8. Close Enclosure

Align the part B and part A to press the device closed. Check that no wires are pinched in the seam. Twist until the mechanism locks.

9. Strap Insertion

Slide the straps through the side loops, fold them over, and sew them securely.

10. Sticker Placement

Apply the final seal face sticker to the front of the enclosure to finish the device!
